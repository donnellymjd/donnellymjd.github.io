Michael Donnelly is a data scientist with over a decade of experience in forecasting, data science management, research project management, and data analysis. Michael is a graduate of Vassar College and holds a Masters Degree from the London School of Economics. He has also managed the development and operations of research units in the public and private sectors. His volunteer work during the COVID-19 pandemic informed major policy decisions at all levels of government; receiving several honors and recognition in national media.
